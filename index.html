Enterprise Integration Modernization

Led the end-to-end transition of a logistics company’s mission-critical integration platform from on-prem Microsoft BizTalk Server to Azure Integration Services—supporting high-volume inbound and outbound EDI transactions across trading partners, warehouses, carriers, and ERP systems.

Business Context & Scope

EDI Modernization

This initiative was an enterprise modernization effort—not a simple technology refresh. It required stakeholder alignment, architectural redesign, operational continuity planning, team transformation, and governance to support critical business operations.

Why it mattered: The integration layer directly impacted operational continuity for inbound/outbound EDI and partner workflows. Migration prioritized reliability and safe cutovers.
